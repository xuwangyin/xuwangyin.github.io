I build models that understand images the way humans do—not just classifying, but imagining.

One model should be enough.

Generative AI and discriminative AI have traditionally been two separate worlds—different models, different training, different applications. But a model that truly understands should be able to both recognize and imagine. Inspired by Yann LeCun's energy-based models, I'm building models that do both—robustly classify and generate high-quality images—trained with a single objective, using adversarial training to shape the energy landscape.
